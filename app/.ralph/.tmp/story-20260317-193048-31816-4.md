### US-003: AssetCard — variant count badge
Status: in_progress
Depends on: US-002

Description:
As a content author, I want to see how many variants an asset has so I know there are more options available.

Acceptance Criteria:
- [ ] AssetCard renders a '+N variants' badge when asset.variant_count > 1
- [ ] Badge positioned bottom-right of the thumbnail, overlaid with position:absolute
- [ ] Badge style: navy background (#001B6B), white text, font-size 11px, padding 2px 8px, border-radius 10px
- [ ] Badge text: '+N variants' where N = variant_count - 1 (e.g. 4 total → '+3 variants')
- [ ] No badge shown when variant_count === 1 or variant_count is undefined
- [ ] Vitest test: card with variant_count=4 renders badge with text '+3 variants'
- [ ] Vitest test: card with variant_count=1 does not render a badge
